Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Free Shipping

We offer FREE shipping on all orders above $99 within the continental United States. Orders below $99 incur a flat shipping fee of $9.99.

Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free for orders over $99)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (Additional $14.99)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day (Additional $24.99)

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing may take up to 3 business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your order through our website or the carrier's website.

Shipping Restrictions

Currently, we ship to addresses within the United States only. We do not ship to P.O. boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or US territories at this time.

Delivery Issues

If your package is lost, damaged, or delayed, please contact our customer service within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

We offer Cash on Delivery for all orders. Please have the exact amount ready at the time of delivery.
